Macintosh Classic

The Macintosh Classic is a computer made by Apple Inc. in late October 1990 and sales ended September 1992. It has a Motorola 68000 at 8MHz (how fast the CPU is).
The Macintosh 128K, originally released as the Apple Macintosh, is the original Apple Macintosh personal computer. It had an base retail price of $2,495 (close to $5,692 in 2016). As the release of its successor, the Macintosh 512K, it was renamed the Macintosh 128K.
